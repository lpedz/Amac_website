History of AMAC

Beginning of AMAC

Association of MACE Alumni Chapters (AMAC) based in the Gulf has been constituted on 13th April 2012 in the MACEAA Global meet conducted in Dubai-UAE as an association of MACE ALUMNI CHAPTERS based in different cities / countries around the world.

Mission

This association is to co-ordinate the activities of MACE ALUMNI CHAPTERS scattered around the world and to identify & form new Alumni Chapters.

Objectives

  • To coordinate under one umbrella & unify activities of MACE ALUMNI CHAPTERS in the Gulf and around the world in General.
  • To Identify and support to create new MACEAA chapters worldwide.
  • To Assist members for better career opportunities, placements & leadership development.
  • To enhance professional and entrepreneurial skills of members.
  • To support activities of MACEAA parent alumni and to function as a bridge between parent alumni and various chapters around the globe.
